What Happened to Your Mercado Libre Account?

Mercado Libre is Latin America's largest ecommerce marketplace — and its enforcement system is aggressive. Accounts and listings can be paused, restricted, or fully suspended based on policy violations, IP complaints, low seller reputation scores, fraud flags, or buyer dispute patterns.

The most disorienting part is that Mercado Libre's suspension notices are often vague. You may receive no clear explanation — just a paused account, restricted listings, or a reputation score that drops to red overnight. Without understanding the root cause, any appeal is a guess.

Each Mercado Libre country operates its own enforcement team with different escalation paths. What works in Mexico doesn't always work in Argentina or Brazil. You need experience with the platform, not generic advice.

Common Reasons Mercado Libre Accounts Get Suspended

Understanding the platform's enforcement triggers is the first step to recovery

Product Policy Violations

Listing prohibited, restricted, or incorrectly categorized products — including items not permitted in your specific Mercado Libre market.

Intellectual Property Complaints

Brand or trademark owners can file IP complaints through Mercado Libre's brand protection program, leading to listing removal or full account suspension.

Fraud or Scam Accusations

Disputed transactions, buyer fraud reports, or patterns that trigger Mercado Libre's fraud detection system can result in immediate account suspension.

Low Reputation / Red Score

Mercado Libre's color-coded reputation system gates your selling capabilities. A red score triggers severe restrictions that function similarly to a suspension.

Identity / KYC Verification Issues

Failure to complete Mercado Libre's identity verification or provide required documentation can result in account restriction or suspension.

Buyer Protection Violations

High rates of late shipping, items not as described, or unresolved buyer complaints trigger Mercado Libre's buyer protection enforcement system.
