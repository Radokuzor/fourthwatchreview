What Happened to Your Google Business Profile?

A suspended Google Business Profile means your business no longer appears in Google Search or Google Maps. Customers searching for your business — or your category near your location — can no longer find you. Phone calls drop, direction requests disappear, and Google reviews become inaccessible.

Google issues two types of suspensions: soft suspensions (your listing is hidden but your account still works) and hard suspensions (your access is fully removed). Both look the same to your customers — you're invisible.

Filing a reinstatement request without fixing the underlying problem is one of the most common mistakes businesses make — and it significantly reduces your chances of getting back online.

Common Causes of Google Business Profile Suspensions

Many suspensions are triggered by issues the business owner didn't know were violations

Keyword Stuffing in Business Name

Adding city names, services, or keywords to your Google business name that aren't part of your legal/real name violates GBP policy.

Virtual or Ineligible Address

Using a P.O. box, virtual office, or address where your business doesn't physically operate is a direct policy violation.

Duplicate Listings

Multiple listings for the same business location or attempts to create duplicate profiles trigger automatic suspensions.

Mismatched NAP Data

Name, address, or phone number inconsistencies across your website, GMB profile, and other directories raise trust flags with Google.

Ineligible Business Category

Service-area businesses operating without a storefront, or businesses in restricted categories, are often targeted for review and suspension.

Review Manipulation Reports

Reported review gating, fake reviews, or incentivized reviews can trigger an investigation and suspension of your entire business profile.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why was my Google Business Profile suspended?
Common causes include keyword stuffing in your business name, using an ineligible address, duplicate listings, mismatched NAP data, or a user report triggering a review. Our diagnostic identifies the exact cause so the appeal addresses the real problem.
How do I get my Google Business Profile reinstated?
Reinstatement requires identifying the violation, fixing it, then submitting a reinstatement request through Google's Business Profile Help form with supporting evidence. Our team handles the entire process from diagnosis to submission to follow-up.
How long does GBP reinstatement take?
Straightforward cases can be resolved in 5–21 days. Complex cases involving hard suspensions, manual identity verification, or escalation to a senior reviewer can take 4–10 weeks. We give you a realistic timeline based on what we find in your audit.
What's the difference between a soft and hard GBP suspension?
A soft suspension hides your listing from Google Search and Maps but lets you still log in and manage the profile. A hard suspension removes your account access entirely. Both require different recovery approaches, and we handle both types.
Can I create a new Google Business Profile while mine is suspended?
Creating a duplicate listing while your original is suspended violates Google's policies and can result in both listings being permanently removed. We strongly recommend pursuing proper reinstatement of your existing profile first.
