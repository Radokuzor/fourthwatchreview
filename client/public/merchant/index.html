What Happened to Your Merchant Center Account?

A Google Merchant Center suspension means Google has detected something in your account, product feed, or website that violates their policies. The moment that happens, your Shopping ads go dark — no impressions, no clicks, no sales.

Google rarely explains exactly what triggered the suspension. You get a vague email, a status flag in your account, and the clock starts ticking. The longer your account stays suspended, the more your competitors take your traffic.

This is not a situation where waiting helps. Appeals need to be structured correctly — submitting a poor appeal can make reinstatement harder, not easier.

Common Reasons Google Merchant Center Gets Suspended

Understanding the cause is the first step toward fixing it

Misrepresentation

Inconsistencies between your website, product data, and actual business practices — the #1 cause of GMC suspensions.

Policy Violation

Products or content violating Google Shopping policies — prohibited items, misleading descriptions, or restricted categories.

Inaccurate Product Data

Prices, availability, or product details in your feed don't match what's on your website — even small mismatches trigger suspensions.

Website Quality Issues

Poor UX, missing contact info, unclear return policies, or weak trust signals on your site that Google flags as untrustworthy.

Unverified Business

Google cannot confirm your business is legitimate — triggered by missing details or inconsistent information across platforms.

Circumventing Systems

Attempting to bypass a previous suspension or Google's enforcement — this is the most serious violation and needs an expert-level approach.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Google Merchant Center suspension recovery take?
Recovery time depends on the suspension reason. Minor policy violations can be resolved in 5–14 days. Misrepresentation or account-level suspensions typically take 3–8 weeks. We give you a realistic timeline during your diagnostic — not a vague estimate.
What is a paid diagnostic and why do I need one?
Our paid diagnostic is a deep audit of your Merchant Center account, product feed, website, and policy compliance. You receive a written report of every issue causing the suspension and a clear recovery plan. Without knowing the real cause, any appeal is a guess — and a bad appeal can make reinstatement harder.
Can you guarantee my Merchant Center will be reinstated?
No ethical specialist can guarantee reinstatement — Google makes the final call. What we guarantee is a thorough audit, a properly structured appeal, and your best possible chance at recovery. If your case looks very difficult, we'll tell you before you spend money.
My account was suspended for misrepresentation. Can you help?
Yes. Misrepresentation is the most common and most misunderstood GMC suspension. It often involves website trust signals, return policy gaps, or feed inaccuracies — not intentional deception. We have specific experience resolving this violation type.
Should I create a new Merchant Center account?
Creating a new account while suspended violates Google's policies and can result in a permanent ban on your domain. We always recommend pursuing a proper appeal first. We'll tell you honestly if your situation requires a different path.
